Well, hello there!

We just wanted to let you know that you're about to enter a RECORDED CALL.

Here's what you can expect:

  1. On entering the call:
    • your display name will be set to whatever is configured in your Zoom profile, and
    • your mic and video will be disabled until you choose to show yourself.
  2. The call will be archived in its entirety privately as a Zoom meeting cloud recording.
  3. The call will be uploaded in its entirety to EDGI's YouTube account as an unlisted video.
    (This means it does not show up in search, but anyone with the link can view it.)
  4. The call will then be shared within EDGI, and added to an unlisted playlist.
    (EDGI cannot make firm guarantees about wider sharing.)
  5. At the earliest convenience, a coordinator will trim the call to the rough start and end time.

If you would like to participate with maximum anonymity, we suggest you instead skip to the phone dial-in instructions below.
